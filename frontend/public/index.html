Fire Safety Tips

  • Smoke Alarms

    • Install smoke alarms on every floor at suitable distances.
    • Maintain smoke alarms and change batteries at least once a year.
    • Ensure the working of the alarms.

  • Fire Extinguishers

    •Place fire extinguishers in easily accessible areas, especially in kitchens, garages, and near exits.
    •Train the workers to use them.
    •Have regular drills to use the fire extinguisher.

  • Emergency Exit

    •Ensure that exits and stairways are clear and accessible at all time.
    Never block the exit with furnitures and items.
    •Practise evacuation from building regularly.
    •Have two exit doors for each room.
